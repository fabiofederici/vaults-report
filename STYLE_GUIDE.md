# Vaults Report Style Guide

## Tone

**Neutral.** No advocacy, no favoritism, no editorial slant.

**Precise.** Specific language, accurate descriptions.

**Institutional safe.** Suitable for professional contexts.

Write as a researcher, not a marketer.

---

## We Don't

Use hype or superlatives ("revolutionary," "leading," "best")

Discuss price, tokens, or investment framing

Accept paid content or sponsored placements

Promote any protocol, company, or brand

## We Do

Describe what projects actually do, in plain language

Name competitors and alternatives where relevant

Acknowledge limitations in our methodology

---

## Directory Entries

One line, 10 to 20 words, neutral, specific.

**Good:**
> Onchain vault creation and governance framework with configurable permissions and policy hooks.

**Bad:**
> The most trusted vault solution in DeFi.

---

## Formatting

Short paragraphs (3 to 4 sentences max)

Minimal jargon; define terms when needed

Categories in Title Case; general terms lowercase

No emojis, no exclamation points
